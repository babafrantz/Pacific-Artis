PhD Progress

This page documents my ongoing PhD research, which focuses on scalable frameworks for reliability engineering using hybrid edge-cloud architectures, digital twins, and multi-equipment model generalization in the mining industry.

Research Focus

My research aims to bridge the gap in reliability engineering literature by designing a scalable framework that integrates different types of industrial equipment into a unified monitoring and predictive maintenance system.

Current Objectives

  • Develop an integrated reliability model across heterogeneous equipment types.
  • Validate digital twin deployment on edge and hybrid systems.
  • Evaluate framework performance using simulated and public datasets.

Recent Milestones

  • Completed literature review on reliability modeling approaches.
  • Established simulation environment for initial model testing.
  • Published preliminary findings in international conference on digital twins.

Next Steps

  • Optimize multi-equipment generalization algorithm.
  • Deploy hybrid edge-cloud infrastructure prototype.
  • Conduct comparative validation with real-world industrial partners.
