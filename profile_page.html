About Me

Hello, I’m Yusuf Oluwasola Omoloja, a dedicated Reliability Engineer with a strong foundation in preventive and predictive maintenance strategies. Over the years, I’ve integrated technology into traditional engineering processes to help industries achieve operational excellence.

My career spans software development for reliability applications, consultancy in Maintenance ERP systems like SAP and MAXIMO, and research in scalable digital twin solutions. I combine hands-on engineering knowledge with advanced system design skills to solve complex maintenance challenges.

Currently, I’m pursuing a PhD focused on creating a hybrid reliability framework that blends edge and cloud technologies in the mining sector.

Outside of work, I maintain a predictive maintenance blog where I share my thoughts, insights, and field stories. I also enjoy writing and reflecting on the deeper connection between engineering, systems, and human resilience.
