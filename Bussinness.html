My Business

As a Reliability Engineer, I provide a full suite of professional services designed to improve equipment uptime, optimize maintenance strategies, and integrate digital technologies into asset management systems.

Preventive & Predictive Maintenance

Designing and implementing maintenance strategies to ensure optimal performance and asset longevity, leveraging vibration analysis, oil analysis, thermography, and more.

Maintenance ERP Consulting (SAP, MAXIMO)

Customizing, deploying, and training maintenance teams on the effective use of SAP PM and IBM Maximo to align maintenance workflows with business goals.

Software Development

Creating tailored applications and dashboards for reliability engineering teams to support data analysis, maintenance KPIs, and real-time alerts.

Digital Twin Deployment

Developing digital representations of physical assets to simulate performance, optimize maintenance planning, and support decision-making across operations.
